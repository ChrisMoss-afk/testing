# Polytechnic Pulse V2.25 Advertising and Employer Inventory Guide

The commercial model should remain useful, obvious, and governed. The hub loses value if paid content becomes indistinguishable from campus updates.

## Paid Inventory

1. Student Deal Spotlight
2. Homepage Sponsor Card
3. Event Category Sponsor
4. Watch Campus Sponsor Card
5. Newsletter Sponsor
6. Standard Work Board Posting
7. Featured Employer Spotlight

## Employment Board Revenue Logic

The Work Board is a stronger commercial lane than generic display ads because it serves a real three-sided need: students want flexible work, employers want access to student talent, and the hub needs repeat-use content.

## Non-negotiables

- Every paid item must be labelled.
- Employer postings must be reviewed before publication.
- The hub should route official career pathways toward RDP Career Services and CEED.
- Sponsors cannot imitate editorial content.
- Student relevance is the filter. If it is not useful to students, it does not earn space.
