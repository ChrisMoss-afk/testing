New revenue lane

Employment postings give local employers a reason to pay for student attention.

The Work Board should remain student-useful first, but it creates a credible paid product: standard postings, highlighted postings, employer spotlights, and category sponsorships for part-time, seasonal, practicum-adjacent, and entry-level roles. The platform should route official career pathways toward RDP Career Services while using Polytechnic Pulse to improve discovery and repeat visibility.

Trust rules

Sponsored content must be useful, obvious, and separate from editorial content.

  • Every paid placement is visibly labelled.
  • Student discounts and local utility receive priority over generic brand noise.
  • No sponsor can imitate an editorial update.
  • Campus partners and paid sponsors are visually separated.
